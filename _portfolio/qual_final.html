Instructions +-

In each annotation:

  • The category name of the sketches is shown in the "Sketch Category" column.
  • Two sketches are shown in the "Sketches" column.
  • Below the sketches, you will describe the objects drawn in non-grey colors in the two sketches by filling in the blank with adjectives / adjective phrases.
